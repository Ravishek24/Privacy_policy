Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28 Oct 2025

Pashupalak ("App") is operated by Animax Health Care Pvt. Ltd. ("we", "our", "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you use the App and related services. By using the App, you agree to this Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

1) Information We Collect

  • Account and Profile: name, phone number, email (if provided), one-time passwords (OTP) for login/verification, and any profile details you add.
  • Content You Provide: listings and media you upload (e.g., animal photos/videos), descriptions, messages you send within the App.
  • Orders and Transactions: items in cart, orders, payment reference identifiers, billing/shipping details.
  • Approximate Location and Address Search: addresses and place details you select via location search/autofill (powered by Google Places). We do not collect precise GPS unless you explicitly provide an address.
  • Device and Usage: device model, OS version, app version, IP address, crash logs, diagnostics, and interaction events needed to operate and secure the App.
  • Support: communications when you contact support.

2) How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and Operate: account creation, authentication (including OTP/two‑factor), listing management, media upload, cart and checkout.
  • Fulfill Orders and Payments: process orders, coordinate payments/refunds, and send confirmations/notifications.
  • Safety, Security, and Fraud Prevention: verify accounts, prevent spam/abuse, detect and investigate fraud or violations of our terms.
  • Improve the App: debugging, analytics, product improvement, and feature development.
  • Communications: send transactional messages (e.g., OTPs, order updates), and respond to your requests.
  • Legal Compliance: comply with laws, enforce our terms, and protect rights.

3) Legal Bases (EEA/UK users)

Where applicable, we process personal data on the basis of: (a) performance of a contract (providing the App), (b) legitimate interests (security, improvement), (c) consent (where required, e.g., certain communications), and (d) legal obligations.

4) Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share it with:

  • Payment Processors: PayU and/or other payment partners to process transactions.
  • SMS/Verification Providers: 2Factor (and/or Plivo if enabled) to send OTPs and verification messages.
  • Maps/Places Provider: Google Places Platform for address search and autofill.
  • Cloud and Storage: Amazon Web Services (AWS S3) for secure media and data storage.
  • Service Providers: vendors who help operate, analyze, and secure the App, under confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal and Compliance: to comply with law, enforce terms, or protect rights, property, users, and the public.
  • Business Transfers: in connection with a merger, acquisition, or asset sale.

5) Data Retention

We retain personal data for as long as needed to provide the App, fulfill transactions, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Media and listings you upload remain until you delete them or your account is deleted, subject to backups and legal retention requirements.

  • Access, Correct, Delete: request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data, subject to legal limitations.
  • Withdraw Consent: where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw at any time.
  • Communications: control non‑essential notifications in‑app or via device settings. Transactional communications (e.g., OTPs) are essential.
  • Do Not Track/Ads: the App does not use targeted advertising SDKs. If that changes, we will update this Policy and provide choices.

To exercise rights, contact us at [Contact Email].

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the age required by local law). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us to delete it.

We use administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your data, including encryption in transit, access controls, and secure storage (e.g., AWS S3). No system is 100% secure; we work to continually improve our protections.

9) International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed and stored in countries different from your residence. We implement appropriate safeguards where required (e.g., contractual clauses) to protect your information.
